Our Leadership

Called to serve, equipped to lead, and passionate about building up the body of Christ.

Senior Pastor & Founder

Apostle David Mugerwa

Apostle David holds a Bachelor’s degree in Christian Ministry and a Master’s degree in Evangelism & Leadership from Wheaton College. He is passionate about church planting, evangelism, and transforming lives through the power of the Gospel.

His vision for God Sent Ministries was born out of a deep calling to reach those who have never experienced the presence and power of God — starting with a simple prayer altar and growing into a thriving global community.

“Our mandate is clear: rescue, recover, and restore.”
Co-Pastor

Pastor Debbie Mugerwa

Pastor Debbie holds a diploma in Early Childhood Development from Concordia University. Her passion is rooted in teaching the Word of God, leading children’s ministries, and counseling women through the most challenging seasons of life.

She brings warmth, wisdom, and a deep commitment to nurturing the next generation of believers at God Sent Ministries.

“God’s love is the answer to every question the heart asks.”
