Our Leadership

Called to serve, equipped to lead, and passionate about building up the body of Christ.

Senior Pastor & Founder

Apostle David Mugerwa

Apostle David is passionate about church planting, evangelism, and transforming lives through the power of the Gospel. His vision for God Sent Ministries was born from a deep calling to reach those who have never experienced the presence and power of God.

Starting with a simple WhatsApp prayer altar, he has led the ministry into a thriving global community that worships both in Crystal Lake and online.

B.A. Christian Ministry
M.A. Evangelism & Leadership, Wheaton College
“Our mandate is clear: rescue, recover, and restore.”
Co-Pastor

Pastor Debbie Mugerwa

Pastor Debbie’s passion is rooted in teaching the Word of God, leading children’s ministries, and counseling women through the most challenging seasons of life.

She brings warmth, wisdom, and a deep commitment to nurturing the next generation of believers at God Sent Ministries.

Diploma in Early Childhood Development, Concordia University
“God’s love is the answer to every question the heart asks.”
Leading Together

United in calling,
purpose, and love.

Together, Apostle David and Pastor Debbie lead God Sent Ministries with a shared commitment to seeing people transformed by the Gospel. Their partnership reflects the heart of the ministry — a family built on faith, love, and unwavering purpose.
