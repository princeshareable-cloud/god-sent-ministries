Who We Are

More than a church.
A family.

God Sent is a Christian Ministries and church located in Crystal Lake, Illinois. This church started in 2022 as a prayer altar online, on a WhatsApp platform and later on Zoom as the need for live streaming grew from our communities across the globe.

God later opened doors to establish a physical church in Crystal Lake. Our desire is to see people saved, healed, delivered and restored back to God.

Our Journey

How we got here

2022

The Prayer Altar

God Sent began as an online prayer altar on WhatsApp, bringing believers together across borders for daily devotion and intercession.

2023

Going Live on Zoom

As the community grew, we moved to Zoom to enable live worship, preaching, and teaching for members across the globe.

2024

Physical Church Established

God opened doors for a physical location in Crystal Lake, Illinois, where the congregation now gathers every Sunday.

Now

Growing & Reaching

With four active ministries and a global online campus, God Sent continues to rescue, recover, and restore lives through the power of the Gospel.
