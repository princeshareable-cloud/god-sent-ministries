Common Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are. Our congregation wears everything from jeans to suits. There’s no dress code — just come ready to worship.

Is there parking?

Yes, we have free parking available at our Crystal Lake location. Volunteers will be happy to guide you when you arrive.

Can I join online?

Absolutely! We have an active online campus through Zoom and WhatsApp. Reach out to us and we’ll send you the details.

How long are services?

Our Sunday worship service typically lasts about 90 minutes, including praise, worship, and the message. Bible studies are about an hour.
